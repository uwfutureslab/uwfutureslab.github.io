Google-UWaterloo Symposium on the “Future of Learning” (Fall 2025)

Join us at the Google-UWaterloo Symposium on the “Future of Learning”!

Futures Lab is an AI+UX workshop, where students work in interdisciplinary teams, using Gemini Canvas, Google AI Studio, and many other Generative AI (GenAI) tools to build multiple bespoke solutions for a learning problem that they have identified.

We are inviting faculty, students, staff, industry representatives and community members to participate in the symposium, to celebrate the conclusion of the inaugural Futures Lab cohort. The symposium starts with a participatory activity where attendees will gather in small groups to have provocative conversations about the role of AI in learning and education. Graduate students and faculty members who are doing cutting-edge research on learning, whether it be technology-related or not, are also invited to present their work alongside the Futures Lab attendees.

The Symposium will take place on December 1, 2025 from 3:30pm-6:30pm in the Davis Center, University of Waterloo.

Program Overview

3:00PM - 3:30PM: Opening (DC 1302)

The symposium will begin with a brief opening talk explaining the Futures Lab and Future of Work and Learning initiatives at the University of Waterloo.

3:30PM - 4:30PM: Provocative Conversations around AI+Learning (DC 1301 / Fishbowl Room)

After the opening, attendees are invited to small group discussions about AI + learning through a participatory activity.

4:30PM - 6:00PM: Futures Lab Demos and Graduate Student Research Posters (Davis Center Lobby/Foyer)

The poster session will showcase both the work of Futures Lab attendees and graduate student research across campus related to learning. Students will be given the opportunity to present a poster as well as demos and other creative artefacts. Presenters are expected to give a short presentation of their work to attendees throughout the session.

6:00PM - 6:30PM: Awards and Closing (DC 1302)

Along with closing remarks, awards and prizes will be given out to the most innovative project(s).
